Logo

Our logo design reflects the main purpose of the app. The leaf and arrow that is in the logo symbolizes the green solutions and alternatives that the app is intentionally built for.
We wanted the logo to have a simple yet modern look and to have symbolism embedded in the logo.
The round logo was purposely chosen to be able to be adaptable on the web.
